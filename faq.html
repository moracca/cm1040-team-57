FAQ

Please see the answers to the following commonly asked questions. If you do not see the answer to your question, please reach out to our customer service line at 1-800-poseidon

  • What are the park's operating hours?
  • Poseidon's Paradise is open from 10 AM to 8 PM daily.
  • Are there any age or height restrictions for the rides?
  • Yes, certain rides have age and height restrictions for safety. Please check our website or inquire at the entrance for specific details.
  • Can I buy tickets online?
  • Absolutely! Tickets can be purchased on our website. We recommend buying tickets in advance to ensure availability.
  • Is parking available at the park?
  • Yes, we have ample parking available, including spots for RVs and buses.
  • Are there facilities for visitors with disabilities?
  • Yes, the park is equipped with accessible paths, rides, and restrooms for visitors with disabilities.
  • Can I bring my own food and drinks?
  • Outside food and drinks are not permitted, but we have a variety of dining options available inside the park.
  • Is there a dress code for the park?
  • We recommend swimwear for the water rides. Please avoid clothing with hard or sharp objects like buckles or studs.
  • Are lockers available for rent?
  • Yes, we offer lockers of various sizes for rent near the main entrance.
  • Is there a first aid station in the park?
  • Yes, we have a fully equipped first aid station with trained medical staff on site.
  • Are pets allowed in the park?
  • Pets are not allowed, with the exception of service animals.
  • Do you offer any group discounts?
  • Yes, we offer discounts for groups of 15 or more. Please contact our group sales department for more information.
  • Can I re-enter the park if I leave?
  • Yes, re-entry is allowed. Just keep your ticket stub and get a hand stamp at the exit.
  • What is your policy on inclement weather?
  • For safety, certain rides may close during severe weather. We do not offer refunds due to weather, but tickets may be rescheduled.
  • Do you have baby-changing and nursing facilities?
  • Yes, we have dedicated baby-changing and nursing facilities throughout the park.
  • Are there any hotels or accommodations nearby?
  • Yes, there are several hotels in the vicinity, ranging from budget to luxury options. Check our website for partnered hotels offering special rates for park visitors.
