Underwater Santa's Magical Village

Join us for an enchanting experience at Underwater Santa's Magical Village! Dive into the holiday spirit as you explore our underwater wonderland filled with festive decorations, colorful marine life, and of course, Santa Claus himself!

Date: April 24, 2024
Time: 10:00 AM - 6:00 PM
Location: Poseidon's Paradise

Get ready for a jolly good time with activities for the whole family, including:

  • Underwater sleigh rides: Climb aboard an underwater sleigh and glide through the crystalline waters, surrounded by colorful fish and mesmerizing aquatic scenery. The sleigh rides offer a magical journey through Poseidon's Paradise, creating unforgettable memories for the whole family.
  • Meet and greet with Santa Claus: Get the chance to meet Santa Claus himself in his underwater grotto. Share your holiday wishes, take memorable photos, and receive a special gift from Santa. It's a unique opportunity to experience the magic of Christmas beneath the waves.
  • Holiday-themed performances by marine animals: Watch in awe as the talented marine animals of Poseidon's Paradise showcase their holiday spirit through captivating performances. From synchronized swimming routines to playful displays, these performances are sure to delight visitors of all ages.
  • Interactive workshops and crafts: Engage in hands-on activities and workshops where you can create your own holiday-themed crafts. From making ornaments to decorating cookies, these interactive sessions allow you to unleash your creativity and take home a personalized memento of the event.
  • Delicious seasonal treats and beverages: Indulge in a variety of mouthwatering seasonal treats and beverages available throughout the event. From hot cocoa and festive cookies to holiday-themed snacks, there's something to satisfy every craving and keep you energized for the festivities.
